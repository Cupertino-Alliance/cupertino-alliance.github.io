The Cupertino Alliance is a micronational organization committed to the development and improvement of member nations.

There are many organizations in the micronational community. So many in fact, they are often referred to as "YAMO"; "Yet Another Micronational Organization". They often exhibit the failure of identifying problems in the community, many of which their members contribute to.

We're different.

We work to stop the ever-growing problem of inactivity within the micronational community. The CA makes every effort to be the micronational organization. We speak sense, and respect your opinion. We have access to useful agreements, documents and resource. Everything we have put into place is for our members. We are a rising star in the micronational community.

By holding multiple sessions per week, we're able to make sure our members have their voices heard.
